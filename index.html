Biography

Sushant Singh Rajput was born on January 21, 1986, to Krishna Kumar Singh and Usha Singh, in Purnia, Bihar. He did his schooling from St. Karen's High School in Patna and then from Kulachi Hansraj Model School in New Delhi. Sushant Singh Rajput was good at studies and cleared 11 engineering entrance examinations in his entire life. In 2003, he secured the seventh rank in the Delhi College of Engineering Entrance Exam and got admitted in B.E. Mechanical Engineering. Sushant was also a National Olympiad Winner in Physics. At Delhi College of Engineering, he actively participated in theatre.He instead wanted to become an astronaut and later an air force pilot but was also interested in Bollywood, being a fan of Shah Rukh Khan.

Why I Admire Him

I was very young when his serial Pavitra Ristaa was on tv. My mother was a huge fan of the serial and enjoyed it. It was only because of Sushant's acting. He was so passionate and hardworker towards the film industry After that his films came and he gained popularity. He was said to be very humble and was a good actor. His acting skills are evident in MS Dhoni and Kedarnath. For the movie Detective Bymokesh Bakshy Sushant Singh Rajput stopped using electronic gadgets and etc as his movie was situated in the year 1942 . He changed his lifestyle and made as if he was living in 1942. It also gave him many losses because since his phone would be off most of the time he would miss many calls for major parties and etc, but he never bothered about these things. Honestly I was shocked when I came to know about his death. My mother, being a huge fan of his, is still very sad. She believed that one day he would become a highly successful actor. So, Sushant's acting and his humble behaviour made me like him. Hope his soul rests in peace.
